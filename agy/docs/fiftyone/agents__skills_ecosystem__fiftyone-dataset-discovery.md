# FiftyOne Dataset Discovery#

Search and load datasets available in your FiftyOne environment by name, tag, or media type.

![FiftyOne Dataset Discovery](https://cdn.voxel51.com/fiftyone-internal-skills/discovery.webp)

## Requirements#

[FiftyOne Enterprise](https://voxel51.com/fiftyone-teams)

[FiftyOne MCP Server](https://github.com/voxel51/fiftyone-mcp-server)

## Usage#

Ask your AI assistant:
    
    
    "What datasets do I have?"
    "Do I have any driving or autonomous vehicle data?"
    "Find something medical"
    "Show me all video datasets"
    "Open the geolocation dataset"
    

The assistant lists matching datasets filtered by name, tag, or media type, then loads your chosen dataset into the FiftyOne App.
